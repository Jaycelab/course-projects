About

Mangata and Gallo is an independent family jeweller with over 40 years of experience and expertise in the beautiful world of jewellery. We are passionate about offering the highest quality of service, and our knowledge guarantees you and your precious jewellery will be exceptionally cared for. We specialize in the finest quality jewellery repairs, remodelling, and custom designs. We take pride in offering a one-on-one, personalized design service that combines exceptional workmanship with meticulous attention to detaill. Immerse yourself in a seamless and educational experience toward the perfect engagement ring as we guide you through the entire process, from start to finish, ensuring your vision becomes a reality.

Products

Our lab-grown diamonds are sourced from various suppliers with sustainability in mind and we are the ONLY fully accredited jeweller specializing in lab-grown diamonds. Once the design has been approved and finalized, the jewellery pieces get sent to our experienced jewellers for them to work their craft in creating your perfect jewellery piece. Once completed, we personally pick up the jewellery pieces directly from our experienced jewellers, bring them back in store and prepare them for pickup.
